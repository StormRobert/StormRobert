## 👨🏾‍💻 About Me

I enjoy building products from the ground up, taking an idea, shaping the experience, then bringing it to life through code.

I work where engineering meets design, so I'm just as comfortable thinking through user flows as I am building them in React and TypeScript. Over the last few years I've contributed to production systems across government, education, fintech and social impact products, always aiming to build software that's intuitive, reliable and enjoyable to use.

When I'm not building, I'm usually refining something I've already built, exploring new technologies or obsessing over the small UI details most people never notice.
